AI visibility by industry

The way AI assistants pick a business is the same everywhere — but the services, the review language, and the sources they read differ by trade. These guides make it specific to yours.

By highlevel.ai · Updated July 2026
This page indexes highlevel.ai's industry-specific guides to AI visibility (answer engine optimization) — how a local business in a given trade gets recommended by AI assistants such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini when a customer asks for a recommendation. Guides currently cover med spas, roofing companies, dentists, law firms, real estate agents, and gyms and fitness studios. The underlying method is the same across industries: keep business facts consistent everywhere they appear, earn reviews that name specific services, be present in the directories and sources assistants read for that trade, and publish machine-readable pages that answer customer questions in plain text. The general playbook applies to any local business, not only the industries listed here.

When someone asks an AI assistant “who’s the best [service] near me,” the assistant reads dozens of sources in seconds and answers with one, two, or three names. The mechanism it uses is the same in every industry — but what it reads, and the language customers use, is specific to your trade. Pick your industry below.

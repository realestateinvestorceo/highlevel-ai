The Gym Marketing Problem

Most gyms rely on walk-ins and word-of-mouth for new members. When they do run Facebook ads or Google campaigns, lead follow-up is slow or nonexistent. Industry data shows that 50% of gym leads go cold within 24 hours when there is no automated follow-up in place. A prospect who fills out a "Free Trial" form at 9 PM and doesn't hear back until the next afternoon has likely already visited a competitor.

Sample Gym Lead Workflow

Here is a complete lead-to-member workflow that a gym can build inside GoHighLevel. This sequence takes a prospect from first contact through trial to membership decision — fully automated.

  1. Ad or search result. Prospect sees a Facebook ad or Google result and clicks through to your "Free 7-Day Pass" landing page built in GoHighLevel.
  2. Form submission. Prospect fills out the form (name, email, phone). They are added to your CRM as a "New Lead" in the trial pipeline.
  3. Instant SMS. Within seconds: "Welcome! Your free pass is ready. Book your first visit here: [booking link]"
  4. Day 2: Email. Send a gym tour video and this week's class schedule to build excitement for their first visit.
  5. Day 4: SMS check-in. "How's your first week going? Any questions about classes or equipment? Reply here."
  6. Day 6: Trainer intro. Offer a complimentary personal training session to increase engagement and perceived value.
  7. Day 7: Membership offer. Present a membership offer with limited-time pricing or a bonus (e.g., free month for signing up today).
  8. If converts. Move to "Active Member" pipeline. Retention automations kick in: milestone celebrations, absence alerts, review requests.
  9. If doesn't convert. Enter a 30-day re-engagement drip with social proof, success stories, and a final offer before archiving.

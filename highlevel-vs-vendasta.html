Company Background: Different DNA

GoHighLevel launched in 2018 as an all-in-one marketing platform built specifically for agencies. Founded by Shaun Clark and Robin Alex, it focuses on providing native tools for CRM, automation, and funnel building. The platform has grown to serve over 1,000 agencies with a clear mission: replace multiple marketing tools with one comprehensive system.

Vendasta started in 2008 as a marketplace-focused platform connecting agencies with service providers. Based in Canada, they've built a network of 250+ products that agencies can resell to local businesses. Their model emphasizes partnerships and fulfillment rather than direct tool delivery.

This fundamental difference—native tools versus marketplace reselling—shapes everything about how these platforms work.

Vendasta White-Label

Can You Use Both Together?

Some larger agencies use both platforms strategically, though this requires careful coordination.

Potential hybrid approach:

  • GoHighLevel for direct marketing services (funnels, email, CRM)
  • Vendasta for specialized local services (SEO, listings, reputation)

FAQ: Common Questions

Which platform is easier to learn?

Vendasta is initially easier since you're mainly configuring marketplace services rather than building campaigns. GoHighLevel has a steeper learning curve but offers more control and typically higher margins once mastered.

Can I migrate from Vendasta to GoHighLevel?

Yes, though you'll need to rebuild automations and funnels since the platforms use different approaches. CRM data and contacts can typically be exported and imported.

Which has better customer support?

Vendasta provides dedicated account managers on higher tiers. GoHighLevel offers community support, extensive documentation, and responsive ticket support. Both have active user communities.

Do I need technical skills for either platform?

Vendasta requires minimal technical skills since partners handle fulfillment. GoHighLevel needs moderate technical comfort for building funnels and automations, though pre-built snapshots help reduce the learning curve.

Which integrates better with other tools?

GoHighLevel offers native integrations with major marketing tools and Zapier connectivity. Vendasta's strength lies in its marketplace ecosystem rather than external integrations.

Bottom Line: Choose Based on Your Agency Model

The GoHighLevel vs Vendasta decision ultimately comes down to how you want to run your agency.

Choose GoHighLevel if you want to: Build and deliver marketing services directly, maintain higher profit margins, and scale with predictable costs. The platform works best for agencies comfortable with hands-on campaign management and client communication.

Choose Vendasta if you prefer to: Focus on sales and client relationships while partners handle service fulfillment. It's ideal for agencies that want to resell specialized local business services without building internal expertise.

For most digital marketing agencies, GoHighLevel provides better long-term value through native tools, unlimited scaling, and higher profit potential. However, agencies focused on local SEO and reputation services may find Vendasta's marketplace approach more suitable.

Consider your team's skills, preferred client delivery model, and growth goals when making this decision. Both platforms can build successful agencies—they just do it very differently.

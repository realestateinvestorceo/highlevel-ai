Why Agencies Are Looking for ClickFunnels Alternatives

ClickFunnels has been a go-to funnel builder since 2014, but the landscape has shifted. Agencies in 2026 are increasingly searching for alternatives due to three recurring pain points.

Pricing increases. ClickFunnels' Basic plan starts at $147/month and only covers funnels and basic email. The Pro plan at $197/month adds more funnels and follow-up workflows, but agencies managing multiple clients quickly hit limits. The Funnel Hacker plan at $297/month unlocks everything, but at that price point you are paying a premium for a platform that still requires third-party tools for CRM, SMS, scheduling, and reputation management. Limited CRM and marketing capabilities. ClickFunnels was designed as a funnel-first platform. Agencies using ClickFunnels typically need to add separate subscriptions for tools like ActiveCampaign or HubSpot (for email/CRM), Twilio or SlickText (for SMS), and Calendly (for scheduling). These add-ons create integration complexity and increase total monthly costs.

No white-label SaaS mode.For agencies that want to resell marketing software under their own brand, ClickFunnels does not offer a white-label option. GoHighLevel's SaaS Pro plan at $497/month lets agencies fully rebrand the platform, set their own pricing, and bill clients directly — turning their agency into a software company with recurring revenue.

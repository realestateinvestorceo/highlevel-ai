VERDICT

Our Verdict

GoHighLevel
Our Pick for Most Businesses

GoHighLevel is the stronger choice for agencies and service-based businesses that need CRM, SMS marketing, AI automation, and the ability to white-label the platform. Kartra has the edge for solo course creators and info-product sellers who need built-in video hosting, helpdesk functionality, and sophisticated behavioral automation triggers. If you run an agency or manage multiple clients, GoHighLevel's unlimited sub-accounts and white-label SaaS mode provide capabilities Kartra simply does not offer. For pure online education businesses that rely heavily on video content delivery, Kartra's native video hosting and membership tools are more polished. The bottom line: agencies and service businesses should pick GoHighLevel; dedicated course sellers should evaluate Kartra's video and membership features before deciding.

Privacy policy

Effective July 13, 2026

The short version

This is a publication, not a platform. There are no accounts, no logins, and no forms collecting personal data. We use standard analytics to understand what gets read; outbound referral links take you to third parties who have their own policies.

What we collect

Analytics. We use Google Analytics 4 to measure pageviews, approximate location (city level), device type, and interactions such as link clicks and scroll depth. This data is aggregated and we do not use it to identify individuals. Google’s processing is governed by its own privacy policy, and you can opt out of GA tracking with Google’s browser add-on or by blocking analytics scripts.

Email. If you email us, we receive what you send. We use it to reply and keep it only as long as needed for that purpose. We don’t add you to any mailing list — we don’t have one.

What we don’t do

  • No selling or renting of personal information.
  • No advertising trackers or remarketing pixels.
  • No collection of financial, health, or other sensitive information.

Outbound links

Links to TownPicked and other external sites leave this site; their privacy practices are their own. Referral links include tracking parameters that tell the destination the visit came from highlevel.ai — that attribution is how referral compensation works, as described in our disclosure.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies for measurement. We set a small session flag if you dismiss the bottom bar, so it stays dismissed. That’s it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access or delete personal data. Given how little we hold, the fastest path is emailing hello@highlevel.ai — we’ll handle any request directly.

Changes

If this policy changes, the effective date above changes with it.

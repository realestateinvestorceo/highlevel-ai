How GoHighLevel Makes Agency Pricing Easier

Most agencies struggle with pricing because their costs are unpredictable. When you are paying per-seat charges for every SaaS tool across every client, your margins get eaten alive as you scale. One extra Zapier task, one extra Mailchimp contact tier, and suddenly your "profitable" client is break-even.

GoHighLevel changes this dynamic with flat-rate pricing. On the Unlimited plan ($297/mo), you get unlimited sub-accounts. That means client #1 and client #50 cost you the same base subscription. Your per-client cost is mostly your time and a small amount of Twilio usage — both of which you can estimate and control.

This predictability is why agencies using GoHighLevel tend to have higher profit margins than those juggling 5-10 separate tools per client. You know your costs upfront, so you can price confidently.

The SaaS Pro Advantage

The SaaS Pro plan ($497/mo) unlocks an entirely different business model. Instead of just offering services, you can resell GoHighLevel as your own branded software. White-label the platform, set your own price, and charge clients a monthly subscription for access.

Most agencies charge $197–$497/mo per client for their white-labeled platform. At $297/mo per client, you only need 2 clients to cover your SaaS Pro subscription. Every client after that is pure profit on the software side — before you even factor in your service retainer.

This creates two revenue streams: the monthly service retainer (what this calculator helps you price) plus a software subscription fee. Combined, agencies regularly hit $1,000+/mo per client with healthy 50%+ margins.

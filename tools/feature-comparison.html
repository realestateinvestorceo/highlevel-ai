Which Plan Is Right for You?

The right GoHighLevel plan depends on how many clients or businesses you manage and whether you want to resell the platform under your own brand.

Starter ($97/mo)

Best for solo businesses, freelancers, and coaches who need a single sub-account. You get the full CRM, funnel builder, email and SMS marketing, scheduling, and AI conversation bot. The main limits are 3 sub-accounts, 1 custom domain, and 1 team member.

Unlimited ($297/mo)

Best for agencies managing multiple clients. Everything in Starter plus unlimited sub-accounts, unlimited team members, unlimited custom domains, API access, and AI voice agents. No per-seat charges mean you can scale without worrying about costs per client.

SaaS Pro ($497/mo)

Best for SaaS entrepreneurs who want to resell GoHighLevel as their own branded platform. Everything in Unlimited plus white-labeling (your own branding), custom client billing, and a branded desktop app. Set your own pricing and keep the difference.

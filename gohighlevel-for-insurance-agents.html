Speed-to-Lead Automation

Configure "Fast Five" automation that responds within 30 seconds of quote submission. This immediate response sequence includes:

  1. Instant SMS confirmation: "Thanks for your quote request! I'm reviewing your information and will have options within 2 hours."
  2. Email with next steps: Detailed message explaining the quote process and timeline
  3. Internal notification: Alert sent to the assigned agent with lead details
  4. Calendar booking link: Automated follow-up with scheduling options

This automation ensures every lead receives immediate acknowledgment, dramatically improving conversion rates during high-volume periods like Annual Enrollment.

Insurance Sales Pipeline Configuration

Structure your GoHighLevel pipeline to match the insurance sales process with these specific stages:

Complete Pipeline Stages

  • New Lead: Fresh prospects requiring initial contact and qualification
  • Contacted: Initial conversation completed, needs assessment in progress
  • Quoted: Formal quote provided, waiting for prospect decision
  • Application: Application submitted, gathering required documentation
  • Underwriting: Carrier review in progress, managing expectations
  • Bound: Policy approved and active, payment processing
  • Active Policy: Client with active coverage, eligible for cross-sell
  • Renewal Due: Policy approaching expiration, renewal process initiated
Important: Configure pipeline automation rules that move prospects automatically based on actions taken. For example, when a quote is generated, move the lead to "Quoted" stage and trigger the quote follow-up sequence.

Stage-Specific Automation

Each pipeline stage triggers different automation sequences:

New Lead to Contacted: Daily follow-up sequence with educational content about coverage types and local market conditions.

Quoted Stage: 7-day follow-up sequence addressing common objections and highlighting policy benefits, with progressive urgency in messaging.

Application to Underwriting: Weekly status updates and document request reminders to keep the process moving smoothly.

Policy Renewal Automation

Policy renewals represent 70-80% of most insurance agents' revenue, making renewal automation critical for business stability. GoHighLevel's renewal workflows ensure no client falls through the cracks.

90-Day Renewal Campaign

Start renewal outreach 90 days before expiration with this proven sequence:

Day -90: Initial renewal notice via email with current coverage summary and market update. Include calendar link for coverage review.

Day -75: SMS check-in asking about any life changes that might affect coverage needs (new home, car, family members).

Day -60: Educational email about market trends, rate changes, or new coverage options relevant to their policy type.

Day -45: Direct outreach with preliminary renewal options and rates, emphasizing early renewal benefits.

Day -30: Formal renewal presentation with specific recommendations and competitive analysis.

Day -14: Urgency messaging about expiration timeline with direct booking link for immediate policy discussion.

Day -7: Final notice with streamlined renewal process and potential lapse consequences.

Pro tip: Customize renewal messaging based on client value and policy type. High-value commercial clients receive more personal touches, while personal auto policies can rely more heavily on automated sequences.

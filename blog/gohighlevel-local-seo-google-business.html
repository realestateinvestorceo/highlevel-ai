Connecting Google Business Profile to GoHighLevel

The integration setup is straightforward, but there are critical steps most people skip. Here's my exact process:

Initial Connection

  1. Navigate to Settings > Integrations > Google Business Profile
  2. Click "Connect with Google" and authenticate with your Google account
  3. Select the verified Google Business Profile locations you want to manage
  4. Enable automatic sync for reviews and messages
Important: Your Google Business Profile must be verified before you can connect it. Google's verification process typically takes 1-14 days depending on your method (postcard, phone, email).

Permission Setup for Agency Accounts

If you're managing client locations, you'll need proper access levels:

  • Owner access: Full control over all GBP features
  • Manager access: Can manage most features except adding other users
  • Site manager access: Limited to basic information and posts

I always request Owner access for new clients, then downgrade to Manager once everything's configured. This prevents permission headaches down the road.

NAP Consistency and Citation Management

Name, Address, Phone (NAP) consistency across the web directly impacts local search rankings. GoHighLevel's business profile management helps maintain accuracy across your primary listing.

Critical NAP Elements

Ensure these elements match exactly everywhere online:

  • Business name: Exactly as registered, including punctuation
  • Street address: Complete address including suite numbers
  • Phone number: Consistent format (555) 123-4567
  • Business hours: Updated for holidays and changes
  • Website URL: Consistent www vs non-www, http vs https

GoHighLevel Profile Optimization

Use the live preview and smart scoring system to optimize:

  1. Complete all sections: Business description, attributes, services
  2. Add high-quality photos: Minimum 10 photos including exterior, interior, team
  3. Select accurate categories: Primary category + relevant secondary categories
  4. Update business attributes: Accessibility, amenities, payment options
Pro tip: The smart scoring system flags incomplete sections. Aim for 100% completion—fully optimized profiles get 70% more clicks than incomplete ones.

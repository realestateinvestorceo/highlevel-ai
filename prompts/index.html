🤖 Conversation AI / Chatbot Prompts

System prompts to train your GoHighLevel chatbot for specific industries and use cases.

Chatbot System Dental Practice

Dental Clinic Patient Assistant

You are a friendly dental receptionist for [DENTAL_PRACTICE_NAME] in [CITY]. Help patients with: appointment scheduling, insurance questions, service explanations, and emergency guidance. Always be empathetic about dental anxiety. For emergencies, direct to call [PHONE_NUMBER]. Services include cleanings, fillings, crowns, implants, and cosmetic dentistry. Office hours: [HOURS]. Never diagnose conditions - always recommend speaking with Dr. [DOCTOR_NAME]. If asked about pain, show concern and offer urgent appointment scheduling.
Chatbot System Real Estate

Real Estate Lead Qualifier

You're a knowledgeable real estate assistant for [AGENT_NAME] serving [CITY_AREA]. Qualify leads by asking: budget range, timeline, preferred neighborhoods, must-have features, and whether they need to sell first. Share market insights about [CITY] like average home prices, best schools, and neighborhood highlights. Offer to schedule property viewings or market analysis calls. Always capture contact info and buying/selling timeline. For listings, mention our average days on market is [NUMBER] and we've sold [NUMBER] homes this year.
Chatbot System Marketing Agency

Marketing Agency Consultant Bot

You represent [AGENCY_NAME], a digital marketing agency specializing in [SPECIALTIES]. Ask prospects about their current marketing challenges, monthly ad spend, target audience, and biggest goals. Offer insights about common issues in their industry. Our services include: Google Ads, Facebook marketing, SEO, website design, and marketing automation. Mention case studies like "[CLIENT] increased leads by [PERCENTAGE]" and offer free strategy sessions. Always ask about their current marketing team and what's not working with their current approach.

Gym Membership Assistant

Our facilities include [EQUIPMENT/AMENITIES]. Classes: [CLASS_TYPES] at [TIMES]. Membership options: [MEMBERSHIP_TYPES] starting at $[PRICE]. Offer free trial sessions and gym tours. Chatbot System Med Spa

Med Spa Consultation Bot

📞 AI Voice Agent Prompts

Call handling prompts for GoHighLevel's voice agent to manage different call scenarios professionally.

Voice Agent Appointment Booking

Appointment Scheduling Assistant

You're scheduling appointments for [BUSINESS_NAME]. Greet callers warmly: "Thank you for calling [BUSINESS_NAME], I'm here to help schedule your appointment." Ask for their preferred service, date, and time. Check availability in our system for [AVAILABLE_DAYS] between [HOURS]. Collect: full name, phone, email, and specific service needed. For new clients, explain what to expect and mention any prep required. Confirm appointment details and send confirmation text. If no availability matches, offer 2-3 alternative times and explain our cancellation policy.
Voice Agent Lead Qualification

Sales Lead Qualifier

You're qualifying leads for [COMPANY_NAME]. Start with: "I'm following up on your interest in [SERVICE/PRODUCT]." Ask key qualifying questions: What prompted their interest? What's their timeline? What's their budget range? Have they worked with similar services before? Listen for urgency indicators and pain points. Based on responses, categorize as: Hot (ready to buy), Warm (interested, needs nurturing), or Cold (not qualified). For hot leads, schedule immediate consultations. For warm leads, offer valuable resources and schedule follow-up calls. Always end with clear next steps.
Voice Agent After-Hours

After-Hours Call Handler

You're handling after-hours calls for [BUSINESS_NAME]. Start with: "Thank you for calling [BUSINESS_NAME]. Our office is currently closed, but I'm here to help." For emergencies related to [EMERGENCY_TYPE], provide emergency contact: [EMERGENCY_NUMBER]. For non-emergencies, offer to: schedule callbacks during business hours ([HOURS]), take detailed messages, or schedule appointments. Ask if they'd like a text confirmation of any scheduled callback or appointment. Reassure them that someone will contact them first thing when the office opens.
Voice Agent FAQ Handling

FAQ and Information Assistant

You provide information about [BUSINESS_NAME]. Common questions include: hours ([BUSINESS_HOURS]), location ([ADDRESS]), services offered ([MAIN_SERVICES]), pricing for [SERVICE] (starting at $[PRICE]), and availability. For complex questions, say "Let me connect you with a specialist who can give you detailed information about that." Always offer to schedule consultations for service-specific questions. Keep a helpful, knowledgeable tone. If you don't know something, admit it and offer to have someone call back with the answer within [TIMEFRAME].
Voice Agent Customer Service

Customer Service Call Handler

You handle customer service for [BUSINESS_NAME]. Listen actively to concerns and empathize: "I understand how frustrating that must be." For common issues like [COMMON_ISSUE_1] or [COMMON_ISSUE_2], provide solutions: [SOLUTION_STEPS]. For billing questions, collect account info and explain you'll have billing specialist call back within [TIMEFRAME]. For complaints, acknowledge the issue, apologize sincerely, and focus on resolution. Always ask "Is there anything else I can help you with today?" before ending calls. Document all issues for follow-up.

✉️ Email & SMS Campaign Prompts

AI prompts for generating effective email sequences and SMS campaigns that convert.

Email Sequence Welcome Series

5-Part Welcome Email Series

Create a 5-email welcome sequence for [BUSINESS_NAME] targeting [TARGET_AUDIENCE]. Email 1: Welcome and set expectations, introduce [YOUR_NAME/STORY], include [FREE_RESOURCE]. Email 2: Share origin story and mission, build trust. Email 3: Provide valuable tip about [INDUSTRY_PROBLEM], establish expertise. Email 4: Customer success story featuring [RESULT_ACHIEVED], social proof. Email 5: Special offer for new subscribers - [DISCOUNT/BONUS] with urgency (expires in 48 hours). Each email should be 150-250 words, conversational tone, and include clear CTA. Subject lines should be curiosity-driven and personal.
Email Campaign E-commerce

Abandoned Cart Recovery Sequence

Write 3-email abandoned cart sequence for [STORE_NAME]. Email 1 (1 hour later): Friendly reminder "Did you forget something?" Show cart items with images, simple CTA "Complete Your Order." Email 2 (24 hours): Address common objections, include customer reviews, offer free shipping if order is $[AMOUNT]+. Email 3 (72 hours): Final attempt with urgency, offer [DISCOUNT]% off, mention limited stock or deal expiration. Include social proof, money-back guarantee, and easy checkout link. Tone should be helpful, not pushy. Each email should be mobile-optimized with clear product images and pricing.
Email Campaign Re-engagement

Win-Back Email Campaign

Create a re-engagement campaign for [BUSINESS_NAME] targeting subscribers inactive for 90+ days. Email 1: "We miss you" approach, ask if they still want to hear from us, include preference center link. Email 2: Share what they've missed - new products, services, or content highlights. Include "catch-up" offer or exclusive content. Email 3: "Last chance" email with special comeback offer - [SPECIFIC_OFFER] only for returning subscribers. If no engagement, final email asking if they want to unsubscribe. Focus on value, not sales. Include survey asking what content they'd prefer to receive.
SMS Campaign Review Request

Review Request SMS Sequence

Write SMS sequence requesting reviews for [BUSINESS_NAME]. Send 2-3 days after service completion. Message 1: "Hi [NAME]! How was your experience with [SERVICE] at [BUSINESS_NAME]? We'd love to hear your feedback!" Message 2 (if 5-star feedback): "Thanks! Would you mind sharing your experience on Google? [REVIEW_LINK] It really helps us!" Message 3 (if less than 5 stars): "Thanks for the feedback. We'd love to make this right. Can [MANAGER_NAME] call you to discuss? Reply YES." Keep messages under 160 characters, friendly tone, easy opt-out option.
SMS Campaign Appointment Reminder

Appointment Reminder SMS Series

Create appointment reminder SMS sequence for [BUSINESS_NAME]. Message 1 (48 hours before): "Hi [NAME]! Reminder: You have an appointment on [DATE] at [TIME] for [SERVICE] with [PROVIDER]. Reply CONFIRM or call [PHONE] to reschedule." Message 2 (day before): "Tomorrow's the day! Your [TIME] appointment for [SERVICE] is confirmed. Address: [ADDRESS]. Need directions? [MAPS_LINK]" Message 3 (2 hours before): "See you in 2 hours for your [SERVICE] appointment! Bring [WHAT_TO_BRING]. Running late? Call [PHONE]." Include cancellation policy and easy reschedule options.
Email Campaign Nurture Sequence

Educational Nurture Campaign

Design 6-email educational series for [INDUSTRY] targeting [IDEAL_CLIENT]. Each email addresses a specific pain point: Email 1: "[PROBLEM_1] - Why this happens and quick fixes" Email 2: "[PROBLEM_2] - Step-by-step solution guide" Email 3: Case study showing real results. Email 4: "[PROBLEM_3] - Advanced strategies" Email 5: "Mistakes to avoid when [DOING_X]" Email 6: "Ready to get professional help?" soft pitch. Include actionable tips, downloadable resources, and social proof. End each email with a question to encourage replies and engagement.

⚡ Workflow Automation Prompts

Plain English prompts to create complex workflows using GoHighLevel's AI workflow builder.

Workflow Builder Lead Nurturing

New Lead Follow-up Automation

Create a workflow that triggers when a new lead fills out [FORM_NAME]. Immediately send welcome email with [LEAD_MAGNET]. After 2 hours, send SMS: "Thanks for your interest in [SERVICE]! Quick question - what's your biggest challenge with [PROBLEM_AREA]?" If they reply, tag as "engaged" and notify [SALES_PERSON]. If no reply after 24 hours, send email with case study and book consultation CTA. After 3 days, if no booking, add to nurture sequence and send weekly value emails. If they book consultation, tag as "hot lead" and send appointment confirmation sequence.
Workflow Builder Customer Onboarding

Customer Onboarding Automation

Build workflow starting when someone purchases [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Immediately: send purchase confirmation email with access details, add to "Customer" list, create task for [TEAM_MEMBER] to prepare welcome package. Day 1: Send welcome email with getting started checklist and schedule onboarding call. Day 3: Send tutorial video and check-in SMS. Day 7: Send feedback survey and case study examples. Day 14: Send advanced tips email. Day 30: Send renewal/upsell offer if applicable. If customer doesn't engage with emails (no opens), switch to SMS. Create tasks for manual follow-up on high-value customers.
Workflow Builder Appointment Management

Appointment Booking & Reminder System

Create workflow for appointment bookings. When appointment is scheduled: send confirmation email with calendar invite, add to [CALENDAR], send SMS confirmation. 48 hours before: send reminder email with appointment details and preparation instructions. 24 hours before: send SMS reminder with address and parking info. 2 hours before: final SMS reminder. If appointment is rescheduled, send new confirmations and cancel old reminders. After appointment: wait 2 hours, then send thank you email with follow-up care instructions. Next day: send review request SMS. If no-show, send SMS asking to reschedule and create task for staff follow-up.
Workflow Builder Event Marketing

Event Registration & Promotion Workflow

Build event promotion workflow for [EVENT_NAME]. When someone registers: send confirmation email with event details and calendar invite, add to "Event Attendees" list. 1 week before: send preparation email with agenda and what to bring. 3 days before: send reminder with parking/location details. Day before: send final reminder with any last-minute updates. Day of event: send "see you there" message with check-in instructions. After event: send thank you email with resources shared, survey link, and next event invitation. For no-shows: send apology email with recorded session (if available) and next event invitation.
Workflow Builder E-commerce

Order Fulfillment & Upsell Automation

Create workflow for order processing. When order is placed: send order confirmation email, create fulfillment task for [TEAM_MEMBER], charge payment method. When order ships: send shipping notification with tracking number and delivery estimate. Day after delivery: send "How's your order?" email with unboxing tips. 3 days later: send review request with photo upload option. 1 week later: send upsell email for complementary products based on purchase history. 1 month later: send reorder reminder for consumable products. If customer leaves 5-star review, send thank you discount for next purchase.
Workflow Builder Membership Site

Membership Engagement & Retention Workflow

Design workflow for membership site engagement. When member joins: send welcome sequence with community access and first-week action plan. Track login activity - if no login in 7 days, send "missing you" email with quick wins guide. If still no login after 14 days, send SMS check-in and offer help call. Monthly: send progress check-in survey and personalized tips based on responses. Before renewal: send value reminder email with stats on their engagement and wins. If cancellation requested, send exit survey and win-back offer. For engaged members, send VIP upgrade offers and beta access to new features.

### Hidden/Add-On Costs

**Twilio (SMS & Voice):**
- SMS: Usage-based per-message pricing through Twilio
- Voice: Usage-based per-minute pricing for calls
- Phone numbers: ~$1/month local, ~$2/month toll-free
- Typical Twilio budget: $15-$80/month depending on volume

**Alternative — LC Phone (GoHighLevel's built-in telephony):**
- Slightly higher per-message/per-minute rates than raw Twilio
- Advantage: single dashboard, no separate Twilio account needed

**Mailgun (Email Sending):**
- Free tier: 100 emails/day
- Paid: ~$0.80 per 1,000 emails (Foundation plan)
- 10,000 emails/month: ~$8 additional
- 50,000 emails/month: ~$35 additional

**A2P 10DLC Registration:**
- Required for SMS in the United States
- One-time brand registration: ~$4
- Campaign registration: ~$15

## AI Voice Agent Details

**Availability:** Unlimited ($297/mo) and SaaS Pro ($497/mo) plans only.

**Capabilities:**
- Handles inbound calls automatically within 60 seconds
- Qualifies leads with up to 3-4 questions
- Books appointments directly on team calendars
- Warm-transfers to human team members
- Supports outbound calling (requires TCPA compliance)
- Supports multiple languages
- Customizable scripts and greetings
- Greets returning callers by name from CRM data

**Additional Cost:** $15-$40/month for Twilio voice charges depending on call volume.

**Setup Requirements:**
1. Twilio account with Account SID and Auth Token
2. Phone number (~$1-$2/month)
3. Calendar with available time slots
4. CRM pipeline configured
5. At minimum 5 test calls before going live

**Compliance Requirements:**
- Call recording disclosure required in all situations
- Two-party consent states (California, Florida, Illinois, Pennsylvania) require explicit consent
- TCPA compliance for outbound calls requires prior express written consent
- A2P registration required for phone numbers

Source: https://www.highlevel.ai/voice-agent-setup

Understanding the 3 GoHighLevel Plans

Starter — $97/month

The Starter plan is the entry point for solo entrepreneurs, freelancers, and small businesses. You get the full CRM, email and SMS marketing, funnel and website builder, appointment scheduling, workflow automation, and the AI conversation bot. The only real limitation is a single sub-account, meaning it is built for one business. If you are just getting started or running one brand, Starter covers everything you need at the lowest price point.

Unlimited — $297/month

Unlimited removes the sub-account cap, making it the go-to plan for agencies managing client accounts. You also unlock API access for custom integrations, white-label capabilities so you can brand the platform as your own, and priority support. If you manage more than one client or need deeper technical integrations, Unlimited is where most agencies land.

SaaS Pro — $497/month

SaaS Pro is designed for entrepreneurs who want to resell GoHighLevel as their own branded software platform. You get everything in Unlimited plus automated billing, a SaaS configurator to set your own pricing tiers, and the ability to charge clients $197-497+ per month under your brand. With just 2-3 paying clients, the plan typically pays for itself and generates profit.

Day-by-Day Quick Start Guide

Days 1-3: Build Your Foundation

Day 1: Complete account setup and explore the dashboard. Don't change anything yet—just click around to understand the layout. Import 10-20 test contacts to work with.

Day 2: Create your first funnel using a template. Customize the text and images, but don't worry about perfection. The goal is understanding how the funnel builder works.

Day 3: Send your first email campaign to your test contacts. Use the drag-and-drop builder to create something simple, then analyze the delivery and open rates.

Days 4-7: Master Communication

Day 4: Set up SMS by connecting Twilio (or LC Phone). Send a test SMS to verify everything works. This is crucial—many trial users skip this step and miss the platform's power.

Day 5: Configure calendar booking with your real availability. Test the booking process from a customer's perspective by booking an appointment with yourself.

Day 6: Create 3-5 email templates for common scenarios (welcome, follow-up, appointment reminder). Having templates ready speeds up everything else.

Day 7: Build a landing page from scratch (not a template). This teaches you the page builder's capabilities and limitations.

Days 8-14: Automate and Optimize

Days 8-9: Create your first automation workflow. Start with something simple like "when contact submits form, send welcome email, wait 2 days, send follow-up." Test it thoroughly.

Days 10-11: Set up your CRM pipeline with realistic stages. Move test contacts through the pipeline to understand opportunity tracking and reporting.

Days 12-13: Connect one external integration (Facebook Lead Ads, Zapier, or your existing CRM). Test the data flow in both directions.

Day 14: Review your analytics and plan your next steps. If you're upgrading, finalize your setup. If you're canceling, export any important data.

Pro tip: Document what you build during the trial. Screenshot your workflows, save your best templates, and note which features you use most. This information helps you choose the right plan and onboard faster after upgrading.

Common Trial Mistakes to Avoid

After watching hundreds of users start their trials, here are the biggest mistakes that waste time and create frustration:

Trying to Set Up Everything at Once

GoHighLevel has dozens of features. New users often try to configure everything in the first few days, get overwhelmed, and quit. Focus on one area at a time—CRM first, then email, then SMS, then automations.

Not Connecting Twilio for SMS

SMS is where GoHighLevel shines, but many trial users never set it up because it requires a Twilio account and phone number purchase ($1-15). Without SMS, you're missing half the platform's value.

Not Importing Real Contacts

Testing with fake contacts gives you fake results. Import at least 50-100 real contacts (even if they're old leads) to see how the platform handles real data and deliverability.

Ignoring the Learning Resources

GoHighLevel has extensive documentation and video tutorials. Trial users who ignore these resources take 3x longer to achieve the same results.

Building Complex Automations Too Early

Advanced automation workflows look impressive but often fail because users don't understand the basics. Master simple workflows before attempting complex sequences.

Not Testing Mobile Experience

Your contacts will interact with your funnels and pages on mobile devices. Always test your builds on smartphones and tablets before considering them complete.

Important: Set a calendar reminder for day 12 of your trial. This gives you time to make an informed decision about upgrading or canceling without the pressure of a deadline.

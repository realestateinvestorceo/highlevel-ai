Payment Schedule and Methods

GoHighLevel processes affiliate payments on the 15th of each month for commissions earned during the previous month. If the 15th falls on a weekend or holiday, payments are processed the next business day.

Payment methods include:

  • Direct deposit (ACH): Available for US-based affiliates
  • Wire transfer: For international affiliates
  • Check: Mailed for affiliates preferring physical payments

There's a minimum payout threshold of $50. Commissions below this amount roll over to the following month until the threshold is met.

Payment delays can occur if your account information is incomplete or if there are unusual activity patterns requiring verification.

Cookie Duration and Attribution

GoHighLevel uses a 90-day cookie window for affiliate attribution. When someone clicks your affiliate link, they have 90 days to sign up and you'll receive credit for the referral.

The attribution system works on a "last-click" basis. If a prospect clicks multiple affiliate links during the 90-day window, the final affiliate link clicked before signup receives the commission.

Important: If a prospect uses your link but signs up through GoHighLevel's direct website or another affiliate link later, you won't receive credit. The 90-day window helps, but doesn't guarantee attribution.

Cookie tracking works across devices if the user is logged into the same browser profile, but doesn't reliably track cross-device behavior (mobile to desktop, for example).

Common Affiliate Mistakes

Avoiding these mistakes increases your success rate and earnings potential:

Audiences quickly identify affiliates who haven't actually used GoHighLevel. Authentic promotion requires genuine platform experience and specific use cases.

Focusing Only on Features

Listing GoHighLevel's features doesn't address prospects' real problems. Focus on outcomes and benefits rather than technical capabilities.

Ignoring the Learning Curve

GoHighLevel has significant complexity. Honest disclosure about the learning curve builds trust and sets proper expectations.

Spam Promotion

Aggressive promotion in Facebook groups, forums, or comments sections damages your reputation and violates platform terms of service.

Important: GoHighLevel can terminate affiliate accounts for spammy or unethical promotion. Focus on building genuine relationships and providing value.

Not Disclosing Affiliate Relationships

Legal requirements and ethical standards demand clear affiliate relationship disclosure. Include proper disclaimers on all promotional content.
